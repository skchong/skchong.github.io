Customer Success Story
UMPSA Overview
Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA) is a specialized public technical university in Pahang, Malaysia, focused on engineering and technology. Established in 2002 as University College of Engineering and Technology Malaysia (UTEC), UMPSA has since expanded its academic offerings, providing a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

UMPSA Challenge
UMPSA wanted to enhance their security posture and ensure their systems were properly hardened against evolving threats. While they had security measures in place, they sought a more structured approach to identify potential gaps and implement industry best practices. Additionally, their IT and security teams were looking for hands-on guidance to strengthen their system configurations and improve overall resilience.

ASK Pentest Solution
ASK Pentest conducted a Security Posture Assessment to identify vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and areas for improvement. We provided a detailed report with prioritized recommendations to enhance their security maturity.
Additionally, we delivered a System Hardening Workshop, equipping their IT and security teams with hands-on training and best practices for securely configuring their systems. This workshop focused on implementing industry-standard hardening techniques, reducing attack surfaces, and improving overall resilience against cyber threats. As a result, the client strengthened their security posture and gained the knowledge to maintain a robust defense strategy moving forward.

UMPSA Feedback

Managed Security Services

Our Managed Security Services（MSS）consists of both Reactive Services and Proactive Services.

Reactive Services will diligently monitor and detect potential attacks in an organization’s IT infrastructure 24x7 and professionally responds to security events and incidents.

Proactive Services minimizes cyber threats by proactively identifying and vigilantly remediate security vulnerabilities in an organization’s IT infrastructure, even before it happen. This is also commonly referred as Managed Detection & Response (MDR).
This can be achieve via:
  • Threat Hunting: By utilizing Threat Intel from global networks and use cases, our consultants will create Threat Scenarios and proactively looking for potential threats within the organization. Example of use case may include Ransomware outbreak, phishing and unauthorized remote access.
  • Compromised Assessment: How do you know if any of your servers, workstations or even laptop from staff has been compromised? By actively scanning these assets using an end-point security agent, our consultants can analyze active process for API Hooking, unsigned device drivers or even traces of hacking tools which could indicate the nod has been compromised.

Security Posture Assessment

Security Posture Assessment (SPA) is meant to establish the current baseline security of the network and systems by discovering known vulnerabilities and weaknesses, with the intention of providing incremental improvements to tighten the security of the network and systems.

Our clients including businesses from Financial Sector, Government sectors and all the way to even small manufacturers. Each client may have their own unique IT infrastructure, design and different critical information to protect. Our Security Posture Assessment is designed to be modular and flexible enough to cater from the largest organization to the smallest.

​

Intelligence-led Pentest

Organizations that required specific Intelligence-led Pentest must first perform Threat Intelligence. During Threat Intelligence phase, Critical Functions supporting systems will be reviewed for potential threat. To further focus on highly probable threat, recent Threat Intel from various sources as well as history of previous attacks will be analyzed. Based on the information, a list of Threat Scenarios will be created. 

The Pentest team will proceed with creating assessments based on the Threat Scenarios. Depending on the scenarios, the assessment may consist of External Pentest, Web Assessment, Data Exfiltration or even Social Engineering.
